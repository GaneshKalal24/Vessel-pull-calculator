3.0 Calculation Results
Stabilising Moment (Ms)
Ms = W × D / 2
Uplift Force (FV)
FV = FH × Tan(θ)
Net Tension in Rope (FR)
FR = √(FH² + FV²)
Recommended Pull Force (RPF)
RPF = FH × 1.5 × (1 / kexc)
Minimum Breaking Force Reqd (MBF)
MBF = 1.25 × 1.428 × (Fcap / kexc)

4.0 Engineering Assumptions & Constraints
  • The vessel will rotate about the defined rotation point.
  • The vessel centre of gravity is approximately coincident with the geometric centre of the base.
  • The vessel is stabilised by self-weight alone. No allowance is made for grout adhesion, shear keys, or bolts.
  • The vessel is sufficiently strong to support its self-weight during rotation and collapse.
  • Pulling is assumed to be conducted as a steady, pre-tensioned pull with no snatching or shock loading.
  • Wind loading has not been considered. Work must occur in light winds suitable for EWP operation.
