Required Horizontal Force (FH) = (Ms+Mw)/A

Uplift Force (FV) = FH × tan(θ)
Net Rope Tension (FR) = √(FH²+FV²)
De-Rating Factor (kexc) = FH/FR
Recommended Pull Force (RPF) = FH × 1.5 / kexc
Maximum Rope Force (FR,max) = Fcap / kexc
Minimum Breaking Force (MBF) = 1.25 × 1.4286 × FR,max
