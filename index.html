Assumptions & Rigging

  • 1. The vessel will rotate about the defined rotation point.
  • 2. The vessel centre of gravity is approximately coincident with the geometric centre of the base.
  • 3. The vessel is stabilised by self weight alone. No allowance is made for grout adhesion, shear keys, bolts, etc.
  • 4. The vessel is sufficiently strong to support its self weight during rotation and collapse.
  • 5. Pulling is assumed to be conducted as a steady, pre-tensioned pull with no snatching or shock loading.
